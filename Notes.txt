
Sales and transaction reports: Summarize sales by pump, product, and time period (e.g., daily or shift-wise), and track individual transactions, including credit sales and payments.
Fuel inventory reports: Monitor real-time fuel levels in tanks, track fuel usage, and manage stock received from tankers to prevent discrepancies and losses.
Financial and accounting reports: Track daily collections, manage accounts receivable and payable, and provide overall financial statements to ensure profitability.
Pump and meter readings: Record and report on dispenser meter readings to verify fuel dispensed against sales and inventory levels.
Employee and shift reports: Track employee performance and manage shifts, including monitoring daily collections and payroll.
Credit sales reports: Track and manage all credit sales, often including details like the vehicle number and customer information.
